# Project Plan

## 1. Project Overview

### Project

Consumer Lending Portfolio Analytics and Risk Monitoring

### Business Domain

Consumer Lending

### Simulated Role

Credit Risk Analyst

### Project Purpose

This project simulates the analytical responsibilities of a Credit Risk Analyst working with a historical consumer lending portfolio.

The project follows an end-to-end analytical workflow that combines portfolio analytics, portfolio risk monitoring, and application risk modeling to generate business insights that support underwriting and portfolio risk management.

The project is developed for educational and portfolio purposes and is intended to reflect practical analytical work commonly performed in consumer finance, digital lending, and banking environments.

---

## 2. Project Objectives

The project pursues three primary business objectives.

### Objective 1 — Portfolio Analytics

Understand portfolio composition, borrower characteristics, loan characteristics, portfolio exposure, and historical portfolio performance.

### Objective 2 — Risk Monitoring

Measure and monitor historical portfolio risk using business-oriented credit risk indicators.

### Objective 3 — Decision Support

Develop a baseline application risk model and translate analytical findings into practical business recommendations that support underwriting and portfolio management.

---

## 3. Project Scope

The project covers the complete analytical lifecycle from business understanding to business communication.

Included:

- Business understanding
- Data understanding
- Data preparation
- Portfolio analytics
- Portfolio risk monitoring
- Risk modeling
- Dashboard development
- Business recommendations

Excluded:

- Production deployment
- Real-time monitoring
- Credit decision engine
- Model governance
- Basel / IFRS 9 implementation
- Model recalibration
- Model monitoring after deployment
